# Hello and welcome to my new website

Hey, I'm Dennis.    

I was born and raised in Cologne, Germany, where I'm living with my wonderful wife and son.    
I'm going to use this space to write about all kinds of things. Most of it will be technology related, but I will also cover various kinds of games, such as video games, card and board games, and TTRPGs. I hope you'll be having a good time, and wish you all the best in the world. I don't have any comments integrated, nor do I plan to do so in the future. I also don't have any social media accounts; I've deleted all of my accounts, so I can better focus on what matters most, and that is my mental health and my family.    

Well, I've already written a couple of posts, so feel free to peruse, and stay tuned for more.
